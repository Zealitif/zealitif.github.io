Welcome to Whisker Wise! A guide for all your needs on cat-care basics and providing resources on how you can lend a helping hand to feline friends in need.

Welcome to Whisker Wise! Your hub for all things feline and feline-aiding related. Here, you can navigate through an abundance of resources that will provide you with opportunities to help cats in need.

Discover our mission and learn why we are dedicated to supporting cats. Learn the basics of cat care so your feline friend at home can feel at ease. Explore our resources to find out how adopting and fostering a cat can be a big help to the community, as well as a chance for you to welcome home a new member of the family. Or simply volunteer at a list of shelters within the local Burke area.

All of this and more are provided here on this helpful site! We look forward to seeing what kind of impact you can make with the aid of Whisker Wise!
